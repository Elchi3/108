Sun Salutations is a practice in yoga consisting of a flow sequence of twelve linked asanas.

Gradually build up to 108!


After pressing "Start", the following voice commands help to keep track of the number of sun salutations in your practice:

  • Say a number or "next" to go to the next round.
  • Say "pause" to halt.
  • Say "done" if you are finished.
